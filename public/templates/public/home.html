Our Strengths

Why Choose NIELIT?

Government Recognised

NIELIT certificates are recognised by central & state government departments, PSUs, and hundreds of top private sector employers across India.

Expert Faculty

Learn from qualified scientists, industry practitioners, and experienced academicians with deep expertise in electronics, IT, and emerging technologies.

Industry-Ready Curriculum

Syllabi updated in consultation with industry bodies like NASSCOM to ensure skills that are relevant, current, and immediately applicable on the job.

Placement Assistance

Dedicated placement cells at each centre conduct campus drives, mock interviews, and connect graduates with hiring partners across the country.

Affordable Fees

Subsidised and government-funded programmes under PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana make quality IT education accessible for all economic backgrounds.

Stackable Certifications

From O Level to B4 Level — NIELIT's progressive certification framework lets you build credentials step-by-step at your own pace.
