Our Strengths

Why Choose NIELIT?

Government Institute

NIELIT courses are recognised by central & state government departments, PSUs, and hundreds of top private sector employers across India.

Expert Faculty

Learn from qualified scientists, industry practitioners, and experienced academicians with deep expertise in electronics, IT, and emerging technologies.

Industry-Ready Curriculum

Syllabi updated in consultation with industry bodies like NASSCOM to ensure skills that are relevant, current, and immediately applicable.

Job Fair Events

NIELIT Delhi conducts job fairs connecting learners directly with employers across IT and electronics sectors.

Affordable Education

NIELIT offers affordable IT education with free NSQF training for eligible SC/ST candidates under government schemes.

NCVET Recognised Certifications

NCVT-approved NSQF courses with nationally recognised certifications accepted across government and private sectors.
