What Happens Next
1
Application Review
NIELIT will verify your submitted details and documents. This may take a few working days. Ensure your documents are clearly legible and correctly uploaded.
2
Eligibility Confirmation
Shortlisted candidates will receive an eligibility confirmation via email and SMS at the contact details you provided. Priority is given to EWS, women, Tier 2/3 city residents, and minority community candidates.
3
Seat Allotment
Confirmed candidates will be allotted a seat in the batch starting 15th July 2026. Seat availability is limited — apply early and check your portal status regularly.
4
Induction & Orientation
Selected students will receive a joining link and orientation schedule before the batch start date. You may also be invited for a Kyndryl corporate exposure visit as part of the program.
5
NIELIT Certification
Upon successful completion of the 120-hour program and assessments, you will receive a NIELIT Certified DevSecOps certificate recognised under MeitY, Government of India.
Your personal information is securely stored and will only be used for program administration under NIELIT's data policy. This is a Government of India initiative under MeitY in collaboration with Kyndryl. For queries, contact NIELIT Delhi directly.
